The Refugees

In 2016, nearly 66 million people were forced to leave their homes due to conflict and persecution – one of every 113 people on the planet. When displaced people cross international borders, they are called refugees. In 2016, the number of refugees reached an all-time high of 22 million people. 17 million of them are under the protection of the UNHCR - The UN Refugee Agency. The Refugee Project allows for map-based exploration of refugee migrations over time. UNHCR data is complemented by original histories of the major refugee crises of the last half-century.
